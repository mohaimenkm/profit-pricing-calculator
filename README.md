# profit-pricing-calculator
A simple yet powerful e-commerce profit and pricing calculator that helps sellers determine optimal selling prices based on product cost, ad spend, shipping, fees, and desired profit margins. Perfect for online businesses and digital marketers.

Here's your E-commerce Profit & Pricing Calculator  🎉
Here's what's packed inside:
9 Tabs of Power:

📊 Dashboard — Live KPIs + quick calculator with margin presets
💰 Costs — 14 cost inputs (product, delivery, packaging, COD, tax, etc.)
📣 Marketing — Facebook, Google, influencer, affiliate costs + CAC
🏷️ Pricing — Selling price engine, psychological pricing, all advanced metrics
⚖️ Scenarios — Conservative / Standard / Aggressive comparison
📈 Charts — Cost breakdown pie, profit bar, margin & revenue projection
🛠️ Tools — 7 mini calculators (VAT, markup, discount, bundle, marketplace, bulk, free shipping)
🎯 Planner — Target profit planner with milestone tracker
💾 Saved — Searchable product history (saved in browser)

Key Features:

BDT default currency (৳), switchable to USD, EUR, GBP, INR
Dark/Light mode toggle (remembers your preference)
AI-powered smart recommendations
Export to CSV / Download report / Print
Works fully offline — single HTML file
